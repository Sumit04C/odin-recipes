Sweet Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of dry oats
  • 1 cup of milk of choice(can also use water)
  • 1 banana
  • 1 red apple
  • 1 heaping tablespoon of peanut butter
  • himalayan pink salt

Cookware

  • Pot and Pot Lid
  • Measuring Cup
  • Wood Mixing Spoon
  • Cutting Board
  • Knife
  • Spoon

Instructions

  1. Lay out all your ingredients and cookware
  2. Cut up your banana and apple
  3. Measure out your dry oats and milk and put them in the pot
  4. Turn the stove on to medium heat and occasionally stir
  5. After 2-3 minutes, add in your cut fruit, peanut butter, and himalayan pink salt
  6. Cook for 2-3 more minutes with occasional stirring, then turn off and serve :)
