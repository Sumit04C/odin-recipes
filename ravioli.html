Spinach Ravioli with Chicken Sausage

Ingredients

  • Frozen Spinach Ravioli
  • Pasta Sauce of your choosing
  • Chicken Sausage
  • Seasoning of your choosing

Cookware

  • Pot and Pot Lid
  • Saucepan
  • Large Strainer
  • Wooden Spoon
  • Cutting Board
  • Knife

Instructions

  1. Take out your frozen ravioli, chicken sausage, and pasta sauce of choice.
  2. Heat up the necessary amount of water in a kettle or on your stove
  3. Put the water in your pot and wait for it come to a rolling boil
  4. While your water is boiling, add oil to your saucepan and heat it
  5. While your water and oil are heating, cut your chicken sausage and add it to the pan
  6. Once your water is boiled, add in the ravioli
  7. Once the ravioli is done cooking, turn off the stove and strain it
  8. Once the sausage is done cooking, place it on a plate to let it cool
  9. Pour your pasta sauce into the pot used to boil your ravioli
  10. Once the sauce is heated, add in the ravioli and sausage and mix
  11. Add seasoning to taste and enjoy!
